# Getting Started

Welcome to DreamTime! This guide will walk you through your first steps with the app.

## First Time Setup

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Open DreamTime in your browser
2. Click **Register** on the login page
3. Enter your details:
   - **Name**: Your name (shown to caregivers)
   - **Email**: Used for login and invitations
   - **Password**: At least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and number
   - **Timezone**: Select your local timezone (important for accurate scheduling)
4. Click **Create Account**

### 2. Complete the Getting Started Wizard

After registration, you'll see a 6-step onboarding wizard:

1. **Welcome** - Brief introduction to DreamTime
2. **Sleep States** - Learn the 4 tracking states
3. **Wake Windows** - Understand optimal awake times
4. **Crib Time Rule** - Learn the 60-90 minute training rule
5. **Recommendations** - How the app suggests nap/bedtimes
6. **Get Started** - Quick tips to begin

You can always re-run this guide from **Settings > Help & Support**.

### 3. Add Your Child

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Under **Children**, tap **Add Child**
3. Enter:
   - **Name**: Your baby's name
   - **Birth Date**: Used to suggest age-appropriate schedules
4. Tap **Save**

### 4. Configure a Schedule

1. Go to the **Schedule** tab (calendar icon)
2. Select a **Schedule Type** based on your baby's age:
   - **3-Nap Schedule** (4-6 months)
   - **2-Nap Schedule** (6-15 months)
   - **1-Nap Schedule** (15+ months)
3. Review the default wake windows and nap times
4. Adjust if your sleep consultant has given specific times
5. Tap **Save Schedule**

### 5. Start Tracking!

Go to the **Dashboard** and use the quick action buttons:

| Button | When to Press |
|--------|---------------|
| **Put Down** | When you place baby in the crib |
| **Fell Asleep** | When baby falls asleep |
| **Woke Up** | When baby wakes up |
| **Out of Crib** | When you take baby out of crib |

## Quick Tips

### For Best Results

1. **Be Consistent** - Track every nap and bedtime, even imperfect ones
2. **Log Ad-Hoc Naps** - Car and stroller naps count! Use the "Log Car/Stroller Nap" button
3. **Check Recommendations** - The app learns from your data and gives better suggestions over time
4. **Use Notifications** - Enable push notifications for reminders

### Common Workflows

#### Starting a Nap
1. Tap **Put Down** when placing baby in crib
2. Select **Nap 1**, **Nap 2**, etc. (or Bedtime)
3. Wait for baby to settle
4. Tap **Fell Asleep** when baby is asleep

#### Ending a Nap
1. When baby wakes, tap **Woke Up**
2. Let baby stay in crib if following crib time rule
3. When taking baby out, tap **Out of Crib**

#### Car/Stroller Naps
1. Tap **Log Car/Stroller Nap**
2. Select location (Car, Stroller, Carrier, etc.)
3. Enter the time baby fell asleep
4. Tap **Start Nap**
5. When baby wakes, tap **Awake** on the dashboard
